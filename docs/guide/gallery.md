# Gallery

A showcase of CatGo's visualization capabilities across materials science workflows.

## Crystal Structures

### Bulk Crystals

- **Diamond (C)** — Classic FCC diamond cubic structure with tetrahedral bonding
- **Perovskite (SrTiO3)** — Cubic perovskite with TiO6 octahedra, lattice vectors, and image atoms
- **Rocksalt (NaCl)** — Ionic crystal showing alternating Na/Cl sublattices
- **Wurtzite (ZnO)** — Hexagonal structure with polar surfaces

### Complex Structures

- **Metal-Organic Framework (MOF-5)** — Large unit cell with organic linkers and Zn4O clusters
- **Zeolite (MFI)** — Porous silicate framework with channel system
- **Layered Material (MoS2)** — van der Waals layered structure with Mo-S bonds

## Surface Slabs

### Miller Index Surfaces

- **Cu(111)** — Close-packed FCC surface, 4-layer slab with 15 A vacuum
- **Fe(110)** — BCC surface, common for catalysis studies
- **TiO2(110) Rutile** — Metal oxide surface with bridging oxygen rows

### Slab Cutter in Action

- **Cutting plane preview** — Translucent planes overlaid on bulk crystal showing the (110) cut
- **Before and after** — Bulk FCC Cu transformed into a (111) slab with vacuum
- **Adsorption sites** — Atop, bridge, and hollow sites identified on a Pt(111) surface

## Spectroscopy & Analysis

### Band Structure

- **Si band structure** — Electronic bands along high-symmetry k-path with band gap highlighted
- **Band structure + DOS** — Side-by-side band structure and density of states for GaAs

### X-Ray Diffraction

- **XRD pattern** — Simulated powder diffraction pattern for quartz (SiO2) with peak labels

### Radial Distribution Function

- **RDF plot** — g(r) for amorphous SiO2 showing Si-O, O-O, and Si-Si pair correlations

## Phase Diagrams

### Binary Phase Diagram

- **Li-Fe-O** — 2D convex hull with stable phases on the hull and unstable phases colored by energy above hull

### Ternary Phase Diagram

- **Li-Mn-O** — 3D ternary diagram with convex hull faces, stable compounds labeled

### Quaternary Phase Diagram

- **Li-Fe-Mn-O** — Interactive 3D tetrahedron with hull faces and phase stability

## Density Visualization

### CUBE File Isosurfaces

- **Benzene HOMO** — Molecular orbital with positive (blue) and negative (red) lobes
- **Electron density** — Total charge density isosurface for a Si crystal
- **Slice plane** — 2D cross-section through electron density of a perovskite

## Trajectory Playback

### Molecular Dynamics

- **Water box MD** — Trajectory playback of liquid water with energy plot
- **Surface diffusion** — Adatom hopping on a metal surface over 1000 frames

### Optimization Paths

- **Geometry relaxation** — Structure evolving during optimization with energy convergence chart
- **Cell optimization** — Lattice parameters changing during NPT relaxation

## Composition & Periodic Table

### Composition Charts

- **Pie chart** — Elemental composition of a high-entropy alloy (HEA)
- **Bubble chart** — Size-proportional element visualization

### Interactive Periodic Table

- **Element explorer** — Hover to see properties (atomic mass, electronegativity, electron configuration)
- **Heat map mode** — Periodic table colored by a selected property

## Multi-Platform

### Desktop App (Tauri)

- **Native window** — CatGo running as a standalone macOS/Windows/Linux app
- **File associations** — Double-click a CIF file to open directly in CatGo

### VSCode Extension

- **Editor integration** — Structure viewer embedded in a VSCode panel alongside CIF file editing

### Jupyter Widget

- **Notebook visualization** — Interactive 3D structure rendered inline in a Jupyter notebook

## Display Modes

### Atom Coloring

- **Element colors** — Standard Vesta/Jmol CPK colors
- **Coordination number** — Atoms colored by number of neighbors (viridis scale)
- **Wyckoff positions** — Atoms colored by symmetry site

### Visual Effects

- **Depth cueing** — Fog effect for visual depth perception on a large MOF structure
- **Orthographic vs. perspective** — Same structure in both projection modes
- **Dark vs. light theme** — Structure viewer in both themes
